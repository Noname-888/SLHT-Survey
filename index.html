Request for Participation in a Survey

This survey is conducted by the International Department of the Japanese Association of Speech-Language-Hearing Therapists (JASLHT) for representatives of related organizations in Asian countries to understand the current status of speech-language-hearing therapy. The survey will take approximately 10 minutes to complete. The results will be published in both Japanese and English on the JASLHT website (this is currently under consideration).

We sincerely appreciate your cooperation.

Please answer the survey on the appropriate page. If you are a representative for SLPs, please respond on the SLP page. If you are a representative for Audiologists, please respond on the Audiologist page. If SLP and Audiologist are the same qualification in your country, please respond on that page.
